Pancakes Recipe

Description

These Japanese pancakes are incredibly fluffy and light, making them a delightful breakfast treat.

Serve them with your favorite toppings, such as fresh fruit, syrup, or whipped cream.

Ingredients

  • 2 egg yolks and 4 egg whites (4 large eggs total), cold straight out of the fridge
  • 1/4 cup (33g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/16 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons whole milk
  • 1 tablespoon avocado or canola oil, plus more for the skillet
  • 1 teaspoon cream of tartar
  • 1/4 cup (50g) sugar

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, milk, oil, and vanilla extract until smooth.
  2. In a separate bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Gently fold the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients until just combined.
  4. In another clean bowl, beat the egg whites and cream of tartar together until soft peaks form.
  5. Gradually add the sugar, continuing to beat until stiff peaks form.
  6. Gently fold the whipped egg whites into the batter in three additions, being careful not to deflate the mixture.
  7. Heat a non-stick skillet over low heat and lightly grease it with oil.
  8. Pour a small amount of batter into the skillet, using a ring mold if desired, and cook until bubbles form on the surface.
  9. Flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  10. Repeat with the remaining batter, greasing the skillet as needed.
  11. Serve the pancakes warm with your favorite toppings.
