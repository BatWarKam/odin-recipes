Salad Recipe

Description

This California chicken salad is a delightful mix of flavors and textures, featuring tender chicken, crisp vegetables, and a zesty dressing.

Perfect for a light lunch or as a side dish at dinner, this salad is both healthy and satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chicken breast, diced
  • 1 ripe avocado, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 cups mixed salad greens
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cooked chicken, avocado, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and feta cheese.
  2. Add the mixed salad greens to the bowl.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil and lemon juice. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  4. Pour the dressing over the salad ingredients.
  5. Gently toss everything together until well combined and evenly coated with the dressing.
  6. Serve immediately, or chill in the refrigerator for 10-15 minutes before serving for a colder salad.
